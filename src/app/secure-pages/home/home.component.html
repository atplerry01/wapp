DECLARATION OF CONFIDENTIALITY

TO: WEMA BANK PLC

I, {{currentUser?.displayName | uppercase}} hereby acknowledge that information/data concerning the Bank, its directors, shareholders, customers, business and affairs coming into my knowledge/possession in the course of my employment with Wema Bank Plc are confidential. I undertake that I will at all times, both during and after such employment in relation to any third party including but not limited to the bank’s competitors (i.e. banks and other financial institution as defined by BOFIA 1991 and as amended from time to time):

  1. Treat and safeguard all information/data as strictly private and confidential and exercise all reasonable care to preserve the confidentiality of same and prevent the unauthorized access to same by unauthorized persons through failure to exercise due care and diligence or through negligence or recklessness;
  2. Only use the information/data for the purpose of properly carrying out my duty with the Bank;
  3. Will not at any time, without the prior consent of the Bank, discuss, disclose or reveal the information/data with or to any other person or party whatsoever other than the bank’s employees and or revelant customer and strictly on a need to know basis and upon instruction by my senior officer/the bank;
  4. Will indemnify the Bank against all claims, expenses, damages, liabilities and losses which may be occasioned by reason of the breach of this undertaking;
  5. Acknowledge that all records in any medium (whether written, electronic or otherwise) including accounts, documents and other papers including private notes concerning the information/data which is acquired by me in the course of my employment are the property of the Bank and I shall use them only for the purpose authorized by the Bank and return them to the Bank on demand at any time and without demand on the termination of the contract of employment;
  6. Acknowledge that disciplinary action/damages alone would not be adequate remedy for any breach of this undertaking and that the Bank shall also be entitled to the remedies of injunction, specific performance or other equitable relief. Such remedy shall be in addition to and not in lieu of or a limitation of other remedies available to the Bank at law or in equity.

CODE OF CONDUCT

TO: WEMA BANK PLC

The Bank’s code of conduct sets out standards which are required to be followed by all employees. Accordingly, a breach of the code will result in any of the following disciplinary measures – HCM Notice of Displeasure, Caution, Warning, Suspension, Termination and Summary Dismissal, whichever is deemed appropriate for the severity of the breach.
Although this code contains brief statements of basic principles together with explanatory notes, it does not spell out the Bank’s operational procedures. The Bank however expects that the actions of all its employees at all times shall reflect the ethical standards outlined by the code with a view to enhancing the good image of the Bank.

EMPLOYEE CONDUCT

All employees of the bank are expected to demonstrate a minimum standard of behavior as outlined below:

Personal Conduct & Data Confidentiality

Every employee must demonstrate a standard of personal conduct at the minimum level stated below:

Each employee shall at all times maintain integrity and objectivity and the highest professional standards during business transactions. Critical aspects of these include:

  • Non-deliberate misrepresentation of facts
  • Offer and acceptance of gratification from customers. Gratification in this context relates to any official relationship induced gift or benefit.
  • Maintenance of absolute confidentiality of all data, banking transactions and customer relationships.

Staff shall maintain a standard of good judgment and moderation in their personal behavior and relationships with colleagues at all times. No staff shall be allowed to consume alcohol and/or controlled substances in the office or during office/business hours. Smoking shall also not be allowed on the Bank’s premises or on official engagements

The Bank will not tolerate any acts capable of embarrassing and tarnishing its image, irrespective of whether these acts occur during official hours or not.

Kindly refer to the Code of Conduct section of the HCM Policy for the full text.
